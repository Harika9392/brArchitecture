About

BR Architecture is a family run Business which has been running for 20 years. Barry Richards has over 30 years of experience in Architecture and has worked with a number of local and national firms in the past on Residential & Commercial Projects. The company prides itself on offering our clients a very Personal Service. The bulk of work comes from Repeat Customers and recommendations proving a high regard of BR Architecture and the quality of service provided. Being a Family Business and understanding the difficulties of juggling Work and Business, we fully understand the need for flexibility and can offer consultations at reasonable times and places to suit you. This approach often proves more productive, and we are more than happy to arrange meetings early in the morning, in the evening, weekends or even after the children have gone to bed.
